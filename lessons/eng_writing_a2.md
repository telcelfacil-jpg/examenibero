# Oxford Test of English: Writing

The Writing section at A2 level usually asks for short messages, notes or simple paragraphs. The goal is to communicate clearly and use everyday grammar with control.

## What a good A2 text includes

- a clear purpose,
- basic organization,
- simple connectors,
- correct subject + verb combinations.

## Useful layout for a short message

1. Greeting
2. Reason for writing
3. One or two details
4. Closing

Example:

"Hi Emma,
I am writing to tell you about my English class. It starts at 4:00 on Tuesday and I really like the teacher.
See you soon,
Ariadna"

## Connectors that help

- and
- but
- because
- then
- after that

## Quick checklist before finishing

- Did I answer the task?
- Did I use complete sentences?
- Did I check the verb tense?
- Did I include a closing if it is a message or email?

## Final advice

At this level, simple and correct writing is better than long writing with many mistakes.
