# Guía Oxford A2: English Foundation (Quick Start)

The Oxford Placement Test doesn't ask for definitions; it asks for **context**. You need to recognize "what sounds right."

## 1. The "Frequency" Rule (Present Simple)

Use **Present Simple** for things you do every day (habits).

* **Keywords:** Always, usually, sometimes, never, every day.
* **Watch out:** She/He/It uses **-S**! (e.g., "She likes music").

## 2. The "Right Now" Rule (Present Continuous)

Use **Present Continuous** for actions happening in this exact moment.

* **Structure:** am/is/are + verb with **-ING**.
* **Keywords:** Now, at the moment, look!, listen!.

## 3. Tactical English Tips

> [!TIP]
> **The "Ing" Trick:** If the sentence has "Now" or "Look!", the answer almost always ends in **-ING**.

### Useful Phrases for the Exam

* "Would you like...?" (Offering something).
* "How much...?" (Price/Uncountable).
* "How many...?" (Quantity/Countable).
