# Essential Vocabulary

Vocabulary in the Oxford placement style is not about memorizing long lists. It is about recognising common words in context and choosing the option that fits the situation.

## Core everyday topics

### Home

- kitchen
- bedroom
- bathroom
- living room

### School

- classroom
- notebook
- teacher
- homework

### City

- hospital
- library
- supermarket
- bus station

## Learn words with actions

It is easier to remember vocabulary when you connect the word with an action:

- kitchen -> cook
- library -> read
- hospital -> see a doctor
- bus station -> travel

## Mini practice

Question: Where do you borrow books?

- A) At the hospital
- B) At the library
- C) At the cinema

Correct answer: **B**.

## Final advice

Study vocabulary in short sessions. Five focused minutes with examples are more useful than reading a long list without context.
